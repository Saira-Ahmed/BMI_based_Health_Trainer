[Exercises]
- Low-impact Cardio: Start with gentle walking or water aerobics, 20-30 minutes daily.
- Chair Exercises: Seated strength and flexibility exercises to build endurance.
- Stretching: Daily stretching to improve mobility and reduce injury risk.
- Consult a healthcare provider before starting any new exercise routine.
- Gradually increase activity intensity as endurance improves.

- Legumes (e.g., chickpeas, black beans - 120 calories per 100g cooked)
- Oatmeal (68 calories per 100g cooked)
