[Exercises]
- Low-impact Cardio: Walking, swimming, or cycling, at least 30 minutes a day, 5 times a week.
- Strength Training: Light to moderate weights or resistance training 2-3 times a week.
- Flexibility and Balance: Yoga or Pilates sessions 1-2 times a week.
- Avoid high-impact exercises initially to prevent joint stress.

[Nutrition]
- Steamed Vegetables (35 calories per cup)
- Grilled Fish (150 calories per 100g)
- Brown Rice (110 calories per 100g cooked)
- Lentils (116 calories per 100g cooked)
- Fresh Berries (50 calories per cup)
